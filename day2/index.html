DOGS!!

Dogs possess an impressive intelligence and keen senses that aid them in various tasks. Their sense of smell, for example, can be up to 100,000 times more sensitive than a human's, and their hearing is far superior. They can use these powerful senses for everything from tracking scents to detecting diseases in humans. As highly social "pack" animals, they form strong bonds with their human families. With proper training and care, their intelligence can be channeled for a wide array of roles, from service dogs and police dogs to reliable guard dogs.
